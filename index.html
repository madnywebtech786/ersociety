About Us
Ebenezer Relief Society

The name "Ebenezer" originates from the Hebrew language and means "helper stone". It is taken from the biblical story in 1 Samuel 7:12. The prophet Samuel erected a stone as a memorial to God's help Israel it is in their victory over the Philistines. Samuel named the stone "Ebenezer," saying, "Thus far the Lord has helped us."

E.R.S Stands for various things depending on the context. One common interpretation is Ebenezer Relief Society, which is an organization dedicated to providing aid and support to individuals and communities in need. The name "Ebenezer" symbolizes a reminder of divine aid and support, reflecting the society's mission to uplift and aid those facing hardship.

Welcome to Ebeneze Relief Society, where we are dedicated to empowering immigrant families as they build new lives in Canada. Our holistic support programs focus on key areas such as education, mental health, and spiritual growth, ensuring that every family feels supported and connected.Together, we can make your journey toward a stable and fulfilling future a reality.
